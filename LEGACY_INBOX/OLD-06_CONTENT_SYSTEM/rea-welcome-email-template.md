## Implementation Notes for MPs:

### When to Use Each Template:
- **Template 1:** Standard onboarding, no major changes from pitch
- **Template 2:** Significant niche or focus area changes
- **Template 3:** REAs who are busy and want minimal communication

### Customization Tips:
- Always include the specific niche and geographic areas
- Use the REA's preferred communication style (formal/casual)
- Add personal notes about your conversation when relevant
- Include your specific training call details

### Timing:
- Send within 1 hour of onboarding call completion
- Follow up with calendar invitation for training call
- Set reminder to check if REA has reviewed their profiles
