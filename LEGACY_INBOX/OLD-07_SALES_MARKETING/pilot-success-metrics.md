# 4-REA Pilot Program Success Metrics

**Last Updated:** 2025-10-22  
**Status:** Active  
**Related Files:** `4-rea-pilot-workflows.md`, `../mp-performance-tracking-guide.md`

## Overview

Defines success criteria and measurement framework for the 4-REA pilot program. Used to validate business model and inform scaling decisions.

## Pilot Program Parameters

### Test Cohort
- **1 MP Playbook Subscriber**
- **4 REA Partners** (2 under MP Playbook, 2 REA Solo)
- **Duration:** 90 days (October 22, 2025 - January 20, 2026)
- **Territory:** Single test market (e.g., Niagara region)

### Success Thresholds
| Metric | Minimum Success | Target Success | Exceptional Success |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|---------------------|
| REA Retention (90-day) | 75% (3/4 REAs) | 100% (4/4 REAs) | 100% + expansion |
| MP Satisfaction | 7/10 score | 8.5/10 score | 9.5/10 + referral |
| GEO Visibility Improvement | +15% average | +25% average | +35% average |
| Referral Generation | 1+ per REA | 2+ per REA | 3+ per REA |

## Core Success Metrics

### Business Model Validation
**Primary Metric:** Revenue > Cost + 30% margin
- **MP Playbook Subscription:** [Amount]/month
- **REA Solo Subscriptions:** [Amount]/month Ã— 2
- **Implementation Costs:** Development, support, content creation
- **Target:** Positive unit economics within pilot period

### Customer Satisfaction
**MP Playbook Subscriber:**
- Implementation ease score (1-10)
- Time savings (hours/week)
- Referral quality and quantity
- Partnership renewal intent (1-10)

**REA Partners:**
- Content implementation satisfaction (1-10)
- GEO/AIO visibility improvement perception
- Time investment vs. value received
- Subscription renewal intent (1-10)

### Operational Efficiency
**Workflow Success Rates:**
- REA onboarding: < 48 hours, 100% completion
- Content delivery: 100% on-time, tier-appropriate
- Cross-promotion: < 4-hour sync, 100% accuracy
- Performance monitoring: < 24-hour data freshness

**Support Load:**
- Average resolution time: < 4 hours
- Support requests per user: < 2/month
- Automated vs. manual processes: > 70% automated

## Technical Performance Metrics

### System Reliability
- **n8n Workflow Success:** > 95% completion rate
- **Error Rate:** < 5% of total executions
- **Recovery Time:** < 2 hours for critical failures
- **Data Accuracy:** > 98% across all systems

### Integration Performance
- **Google Workspace Sync:** < 15 minute delay
- **Slack Notifications:** < 5 minute delivery
- **Email Delivery:** > 99% success rate
- **API Response Times:** < 2 seconds average

### Partnership Health
- REA-MP communication frequency and quality
- Cross-promotion implementation enthusiasm
- Willingness to provide testimonials
- Referral of other potential partners

### Content Effectiveness
- GEO/AIO visibility score improvements
- Social media engagement rates
- Website traffic from content

### Process Improvements
- Identified workflow optimizations
- Automation opportunities discovered
- Support process efficiencies
- Documentation gaps addressed

## Measurement Framework

### Daily Tracking
- Workflow execution logs and errors
- Content implementation rates
- System performance metrics
- Support request volume and type

### Weekly Analysis
- Customer satisfaction check-ins
- GEO/AIO visibility score trends
- Referral tracking and quality assessment
- Process improvement identification

### Monthly Deep Dives
- Comprehensive performance review
- Financial analysis (revenue vs. costs)
- Customer retention and satisfaction
- Scaling readiness assessment

## Decision Gates

### Continue to Scale (All metrics met)
- Proceed with additional MP onboarding
- Expand to new territories
- Invest in enhanced features

### Iterate and Improve (Most metrics met)
- Address specific identified issues
- Extend pilot by 30-60 days
- Refine processes before scaling

### Pivot or Stop (Critical metrics failed)
- Fundamental business model issues
- Customer satisfaction below threshold
- Operational costs exceeding projections

## Reporting Structure

### Internal Reports
- **Weekly Status:** Team meeting, 30 minutes
- **Monthly Review:** Executive summary, 60 minutes
- **Pilot Completion:** Comprehensive analysis, recommendations

### External Communications
- **MP Updates:** Bi-weekly performance summary
- **REA Updates:** Monthly visibility reports
- **Stakeholder Updates:** Monthly progress highlights

**Success Definition:** The pilot is successful if we validate the business model, demonstrate customer satisfaction, and identify a clear path to profitable scaling with manageable support loads.
