# DGA Business Model

## Subscription Streams

### Playbook Subscriptions (Mortgage Professionals)
- **Starter:** $249/month - 1-3 playbook partners
- **Pro:** $499/month - 4-6 playbook partners each  
- **Elite:** $999/month - 7-10 playbook partners each

### Solo Subscriptions (Individual Professionals)  
- **REA Solo:** $249/month - Territory + Niche exclusivity
- **MP Solo:** $249/month - Territory + Niche exclusivity

## Revenue Projections
- 78 Ontario territories × 11 REA niches = 858 REA solo slots
- 78 Ontario territories × 8 MP niches = 624 MP solo slots
- 78 Playbook territory slots (varying tiers)

## Value Proposition
- AI-powered visibility audits (GEO + AIO scoring)
- 30-day recurring content plans
- Territory-based exclusivity
- Automated proof reporting
