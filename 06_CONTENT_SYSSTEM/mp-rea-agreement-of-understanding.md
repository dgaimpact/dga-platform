## Purpose

This Agreement of Understanding outlines the mutual commitments between [MP Name] and [REA Name] in our partnership through the DGA Playbook stream. The goal is to create a transparent, productive relationship that drives visibility and leads for both parties.

## Our Partnership at a Glance

- **MP Investment:** [MP Name] invests in creating and providing monthly content (including a 3,000+ word foundation article and promotional materials) to increase [REA Name]'s visibility in Google and AI search results.
- **REA Commitment:** [REA Name] commits to implementing the provided content and referring mortgage leads to [MP Name] as their exclusive mortgage partner for leads generated through this content.

## Mutual Commitments

### Mortgage Professional ([MP Name]) Commitments

1. **Content Creation**
   - Provide a monthly 3,000+ word foundation article tailored to [REA Name]'s niche and market.
   - Create supporting content (social media posts, GBP posts, email newsletters) for the chosen tier ([High/Medium/Low]).
   - Ensure all content is optimized for GEO/AIO visibility and includes proper schema markup.

2. **Visibility Investment**
   - Cover the cost of content creation and strategy as part of the DGA Playbook subscription.
   - Monitor and report on visibility improvements and AI search appearances.

3. **Partnership Support**
   - Provide training and support for content implementation.
   - Schedule regular check-ins to discuss performance and adjustments.

### Real Estate Agent ([REA Name]) Commitments

1. **Content Implementation**
   - Publish the provided content according to the monthly content calendar.
   - Use the recommended images, posting times, and platform-specific best practices.
   - Maintain consistent branding and messaging across all channels.

2. **Exclusive Referral Partnership**
   - Refer all mortgage leads generated from the DGA content to [MP Name] as the exclusive mortgage partner.
   - Promote the partnership by cross-linking to [MP Name]'s content and tagging in social media posts.

3. **Communication & Responsiveness**
   - Respond to [MP Name]'s communication within 24-48 hours.
   - Participate in scheduled check-ins and performance reviews.
   - Provide feedback on content and lead quality.

## Performance Expectations

### Content Implementation
- **Target:** [REA Name] will implement [X]% of provided content monthly (based on tier).
- **Review:** Implementation performance will be reviewed monthly.

### Referral Flow
- **Expectation:** [REA Name] will refer an average of [X] leads per month from the content.
- **Review:** Referral quantity and quality will be reviewed quarterly.

### Communication
- **Expectation:** Both parties will maintain open and timely communication.
- **Review:** Communication responsiveness will be monitored continuously.

## Performance Review Process

1. **30-Day Check-in:** Review initial implementation and communication.
2. **90-Day Performance Review:** Assess referral flow and partnership value.
3. **Ongoing Quarterly Reviews:** Evaluate partnership health and adjust strategies.

## Partnership Continuation

This partnership is based on mutual value. If, after 90 days, the partnership is not delivering value for both parties, we will discuss adjustments. If performance does not improve, the partnership may be discontinued with 30 days' notice.
