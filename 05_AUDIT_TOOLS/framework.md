# DGA Audit Framework

The Digital Growth Accelerator (DGA) Audit analyzes a REALTOR® or Mortgage Professional’s visibility across AI-driven discovery platforms.

## Core Pillars
1. **Visibility Score** – measures AI discoverability across Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews.
2. **Content Presence** – evaluates consistency, structure, and alignment of digital content with generative search expectations.
3. **Authority & Reviews** – measures trust signals such as testimonials, media coverage, and local SEO.
4. **Engagement & Conversion** – assesses the strength of calls-to-action, tracking, and remarketing touchpoints.
5. **Recommendations** – prioritized 30-day actions for improving AI visibility.

Each audit should conclude with:
- A **Visibility Summary (scorecard)**
- **Priority Fixes (ranked by impact)**
- **Next 3 Recommended Actions**
