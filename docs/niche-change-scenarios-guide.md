# Niche Change Scenarios Guide

**Last Updated:** 2025-10-22  
**Status:** Active  
**Related Files:** `templates/rea-onboarding-capture-form.md`, `docs/rea-onboarding-checklist.md`

## Overview

This guide provides Mortgage Professionals with quick-reference strategies for handling common niche change scenarios during REA onboarding. Ensures smooth transitions when REA's actual focus differs from initial assumptions.

## Quick-Reference Decision Matrix

| Scenario | Impact Level | Action Required | Timeline Impact |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Minor refinement (e.g., "luxury" → "luxury condos") | Low | Content adjustment | 0-1 days |
| Moderate shift (e.g., "first-time buyers" → "investment properties") | Medium | Strategy update + content rewrite | 1-2 days |
| Major pivot (e.g., "residential" → "commercial") | High | Full re-audit + new strategy | 3-5 days |
| Multiple niches requested | High | Priority selection required | 2-3 days |

## Scenario 1: Minor Niche Refinement

### Situation
REA agrees with general direction but wants more specific focus.

**Example:** 
- Pitch: "Luxury properties"
- Actual: "Luxury waterfront properties"

### MP Response Script
"That's actually perfect - being more specific helps us dominate a more targeted search area. Luxury waterfront is actually easier to rank for than general luxury. This is a great refinement."

### Actions Required
- [ ] Update niche in tracking sheet
- [ ] Adjust content keywords to reflect specificity
- [ ] No delay to content delivery timeline
- [ ] Proceed with standard onboarding

---

## Scenario 2: Moderate Niche Shift

### Situation
REA wants different but related niche focus.

**Example:**
- Pitch: "First-time home buyers" 
- Actual: "Move-up families"

### MP Response Script
"I understand - move-up families have different needs and search patterns than first-time buyers. This requires some strategy adjustment, but we can have your updated content plan ready within 48 hours."

### Actions Required
- [ ] Pause n8n workflow temporarily
- [ ] Conduct quick keyword audit for new niche
- [ ] Update content strategy and calendar
- [ ] Revised delivery timeline: +2 days
- [ ] Communicate new timeline clearly

---

## Scenario 3: Major Niche Pivot

### Situation
REA wants completely different specialty area.

**Example:**
- Pitch: "Residential resale"
- Actual: "New construction/pre-construction"

### MP Response Script
"This is a significant shift from our initial focus. New construction requires different content, different search terms, and different audience targeting. I'll need to run a completely new audit, which will delay your first content delivery by 3-5 days. Are you comfortable with that timeline?"

### Actions Required
- [ ] Halt all current content development
- [ ] Conduct full niche audit for new focus area
- [ ] Rebuild content strategy from ground up
- [ ] Revised delivery timeline: +3-5 days
- [ ] Confirm REA commitment to new direction

---

## Scenario 4: Multiple Niches Requested

### Situation
REA wants to cover several specialties simultaneously.

**Example:**
"I work with first-time buyers, investors, AND luxury clients"

### MP Response Script
"I appreciate that you have diverse experience, but trying to dominate multiple niches simultaneously dilutes our effectiveness. Google's AI rewards specialists, not generalists. Let's start with your strongest niche for the first 90 days, then evaluate adding a second niche once we've established dominance in the first one."

### Alternative Approach (If REA insists)
"If you're set on multiple niches, we can rotate focus monthly, but this will slow overall visibility growth. The data shows specialists achieve 3x faster visibility than generalists."

### Actions Required
- [ ] Guide REA to prioritize primary niche
- [ ] If multiple niches proceed: create rotating content calendar
- [ ] Set clear expectations about slower results
- [ ] Schedule 90-day review to evaluate approach

---

## Scenario 5: Geographic Focus Change

### Situation
REA wants different geographic coverage than assumed.

**Example:**
- Pitch: "Niagara Falls core"
- Actual: "Whole Niagara region"

### MP Response Script
"Expanding to the whole Niagara region changes our competitive landscape significantly. We'll need to adjust our local SEO strategy and may face more competition, but the broader reach could be valuable if you have the capacity to serve that larger area."

### Actions Required
- [ ] Verify territory availability for expanded area
- [ ] Adjust local keyword strategy
- [ ] Update schema markup for broader region
- [ ] Minor timeline impact: +1 day

## Emergency Response Protocol

### Red Flags - When to Reconsider Partnership
- **Niche hopping:** REA changes focus multiple times in conversation
- **Unrealistic expectations:** "I want to rank for everything"
- **Lack of commitment:** "Let's try this and see what happens"
- **Brand confusion:** Cannot clearly articulate their specialty

### Exit Script if Needed
"I'm concerned we don't have a clear direction for your content. Without a focused niche, our visibility efforts won't be effective. Let's pause and reconnect when you have a clearer picture of your specialty focus."

---

## Pro Tips for MPs

### Prevention Strategies
- **Better pre-qualification:** Ask about niche focus before creating pitch audit
- **Multiple option pitches:** "I can show you audits for luxury OR investment properties"
- **Clear value explanation:** "Specialists get 3x more AI recommendations"

### Communication Best Practices
- **Stay positive:** Frame changes as "refinements" not "mistakes"
- **Set clear timelines:** Always specify delivery impact immediately
- **Get confirmation:** "So we're moving forward with [new niche] and [new timeline]?"
- **Document everything:** Update tracking sheets in real-time

### When to Escalate
- Geographic expansion into unavailable territories
- REA requesting niches outside their actual expertise
- Multiple major changes in single onboarding
- Compliance concerns with new niche focus

## Success Metrics for Niche Changes

- **Adjustment accuracy:** How well new content matches REA's actual business
- **Timeline adherence:** Meeting revised delivery commitments
- **REA satisfaction:** Post-change confidence in partnership
- **Visibility results:** GEO/AIO score improvements with adjusted focus

---

**Remember:** Better to delay content by a few days and get the niche right than deliver quickly with the wrong focus.
