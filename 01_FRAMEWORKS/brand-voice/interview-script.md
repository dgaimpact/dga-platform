# Brand Voice Interview Script  
*A structured interview for capturing the client's authentic voice, tone, language, and messaging DNA.*

## Purpose
This interview defines the client’s Brand Voice Blueprint (BVB).  
It ensures all content, messaging, and GEO signals reflect the client’s authentic style across every DGA framework (OCGF, GEO, onboarding, audits, and automations).

---

# SECTION 1 — Introduction & Context

## 1.1 Purpose Statement (Read to Client)
“Before we create any content or messaging, we document your brand voice — how you naturally communicate and how you want to sound across every marketing channel. This ensures everything DGA produces feels authentically like you.”

## 1.2 What We'll Cover
- Your personality and communication style  
- Your audience and niche  
- Your tone across situations  
- Words and phrases you prefer  
- Words or tones you want to avoid  
- How your story influences your voice  
- Examples that “sound like you” and ones that don’t  

---

# SECTION 2 — Brand Personality Discovery

## 2.1 Core Personality Questions
- How would you describe your communication style in three words?  
- How do clients typically describe you?  
- When you speak, do you naturally lean more formal or casual?  
- Are you more direct and concise, or descriptive and detailed?  
- Which emotions do you want your brand to project? (confident, calm, friendly, expert, approachable, etc.)

## 2.2 Professional Identity
- What do you want people to feel after interacting with your content?  
- What do you never want people to feel?  

---

# SECTION 3 — Audience & Niche Alignment

## 3.1 Target Audience
- Who is your primary audience or niche?  
- What do they value most in communication?  
- What frustrates them in your industry?  
- What “tone” resonates with them best? (confidence, clarity, friendliness, authority)

## 3.2 Market Positioning
- How do you want to be perceived in your local market?  
- What do you want to be known for?  
- What differentiates you from competitors?

---

# SECTION 4 — Messaging DNA

## 4.1 Signature Language
- What words or phrases do you use often with clients?  
- What phrases do clients repeat back to you?  
- What industry terms do you prefer?  
- What terms do you avoid because they feel wrong or overused?

## 4.2 Strategic Messaging
- What message themes are core to your brand?  
- What topics or angles do you find yourself repeating often?  

---

# SECTION 5 — Language Preferences

## 5.1 Preferred Language
- Do you prefer shorter, punchier sentences or longer, structured explanations?  
- Do you like analogies or prefer direct statements?  
- Do you want content to feel educational, inspirational, authoritative, or conversational?

## 5.2 Words to Avoid
- Are there any overused industry clichés you dislike?  
- Are there any words or tones that *absolutely* do not feel like you?

---

# SECTION 6 — Content Style

## 6.1 Formatting
- Do you prefer content that is:  
  - Bullet-heavy?  
  - Narrative?  
  - Step-by-step instructional?  
  - Story-led?

## 6.2 CTAs and Persuasion
- Do you like strong, clear calls to action?  
- Or soft, advisory-style CTAs?  
- Do you want content to guide, tell, or recommend?

---

# SECTION 7 — Examples & Counterexamples

## 7.1 Examples to Collect
Ask the client for:

- 2–3 social posts they love  
- 2–3 posts they dislike  
- 1–2 newsletters or emails they think represent their voice  
- Website sections or bios they feel are accurate  

## 7.2 Analysis Questions
For each example:

- What do you like about it?  
- What feels aligned with your voice?  
- What doesn’t feel aligned?  
- What elements should we replicate?  
- What should we avoid?

---

# SECTION 8 — Brand Stories & Anchors

## 8.1 Story Discovery
- What’s your “why” for working in your profession?  
- What’s a story that shaped the way you operate today?  
- What do clients often thank you for?  
- What makes you proud of your work?  

## 8.2 Trust and Differentiation
- What values guide your decision-making?  
- What makes you an expert or specialist in your niche?

---

# SECTION 9 — Final Calibration

## 9.1 Confirm Alignment
“Here’s what I’m hearing so far…”  
(Summarize: tone, traits, language, style)

Ask:  
- Does this sound right?  
- What would you change or clarify?  
- Are there any missing pieces?

## 9.2 BVB Delivery Expectations
Explain next steps:
- You will receive a Brand Voice Blueprint  
- All content will follow these standards  
- Any future shifts can be updated in the BVB  

---

# END OF INTERVIEW SCRIPT
This script supports the creation of the Brand Voice Blueprint and integrates across DGA’s content, onboarding, GEO, and engagement systems.
