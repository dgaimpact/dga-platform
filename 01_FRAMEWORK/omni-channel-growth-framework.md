## **Implementation Best Practices**

### **Publication Timing**

* Publish foundation articles **simultaneously**
* Schedule supporting content according to tier
* Stagger social posts for ongoing visibility
* Maintain consistent cadence

### **Brand Voice Consistency**

* Verify all content matches the Brand Voice Blueprint
* Apply tone adjustments for platform-specific formats
* Ensure derivative content retains core personality and language preferences

### **Cross-Promotion Excellence**

* Verify all hyperlinks
* Use natural anchor text
* Tag partners where appropriate
* Share partner content for mutual amplification

### **GEO/AIO Optimization**

* Implement schema markup
* Follow platform best practices
* Maintain NAP consistency
* Update GBP and profiles regularly

## **Common Scenarios & Solutions**

### **Publication Conflicts**

**Problem:** Partner cannot publish simultaneously
**Solution:** Publish within 24 hours; update links as needed

### **Multiple REA Partners**

**Problem:** MP receiving multiple plans in same cycle
**Solution:** Stagger publication; keep niche focus distinct

### **Tier Changes**

**Problem:** Subscriber changes volume tier
**Solution:** Pro-rate delivery; adjust calendar accordingly
